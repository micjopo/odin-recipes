Fruit Smoothie Recipe

Description

Let's make a fruit smoothie! Cook time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 blender
  • 1 cup of chopped frozen fruit
  • Ice
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend all ingredients until desired consistency.
  3. Serve in a chilled mason jar and straw.
