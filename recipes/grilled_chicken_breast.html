Grilled Chicken Breast Recipe

Description

Here's my home-cooked recipe of grilled chicken breast. It's a very straightforward recipe and should take you no longer than a half-hour to cook. Cook time: 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1lb fair trade chicken breast
  • 1x garlic clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1x fresh rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspsoon of olive oil
  • Salt for brining
  • Parchment paper
  • Oven-safe cooking dish

Steps

  1. Brine the chicken and let it sit for 24 hours in the refridgerator.
  2. After 24 hours have passed, take the chicken out.
  3. Preheat the oven for 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Combine all the ingredients and apply them onto the chicken.
  5. Place the chicken into the oven and cook it for 25 minutes or until the chicken is tender and golden brown.
  6. After cooking the chicken, take it out of the oven and let it sit for five minutes to cool.
  7. Serve with your sauce of choice.
