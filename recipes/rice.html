White Rice Recipe

Description

This recipe guides you on how to cook short grain white rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup short grain white rice
  • A rice cooker pot
  • 1 and 1/4 water for cooking the rice

Steps

  1. Pour one cup of uncooked rice into the rice pot.
  2. Pour about two cups of water and wash the rice.
  3. Rinse the water out of the rice pot.
  4. Pour one and 1/4 rice into the pot.
  5. Cook the rice in a rice cooker or on top of the oven.
  6. Serve the rice in your favorite rice bowl and with grilled chicken breast.
